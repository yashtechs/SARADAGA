I cannot locate all of my accounts. Where can I find them?

You will only find accounts with the following features in Easy banking:

  • Only individual current and savings accounts of which you are the holder or a proxy. You can still view all other accounts such as time deposits, professional accounts etc. in PC banking.
  • Any accounts you have hidden in PC banking will also be hidden in Easy banking. If you would like to change this, you can do so via "Your preferences" > "Hide accounts" in PC banking.
